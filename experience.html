Experience

 
Elliott Valuation & Consulting Services, LLC is a fullMservice commercial real estate appraisal and advisory firm completing a wide range of projects primarily in the southeast. Mr. Elliott’s passion for real estate began in the early 2000’s and has continued to blossom with an eagerness to assist people in realMworld problems by providing realMworld solutions. His career started as a staff appraiser for the Spartanburg County Assessor’s Office and has given him a strong foundation for the South Carolina tax code. From there, he has served as a senior associate and partner at other firms handling client needs and dayMtoMday tasks running a business. He has completed specialized and complex assignments throughout the southeast and as far west as Nebraska. Mr. Elliott has become a leader within the appraisal industry and served in numerous capacities to further the profession. Mr. Elliott was elected as a discussion leader for the Appraisal Institute’s Leadership Development & Advisory Council in 2014 and also presented a seminar at the AI’s national meeting. He is also a facilitator for the Capstone Program of the AI, which mentors, advises and grades candidates for their MAI designation requirement of the demonstration report.
