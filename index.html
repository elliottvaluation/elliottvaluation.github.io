Elliott Valuation & Consulting Services, LLC is a full service commercial real estate advisory firm that specializes in valuation and consulting assignments. While the valuation process reports a single number, the base root of our services revolve around problem solving. Each client has a specific need or issue and we can assist and supply answers in a variety of ways. We are firm believers in prompt communication, individualized customer service and going the extra mile to meet your needs. Our passion for service and real estate drives the company forward as well as our dedication to research and extensive market knowledge. Our wide range of market knowledge allows our clients to depend on us for most any property type or project.

Robert Rhyne Elliott

Owner, Principal
Rob Elliott, MAI, SRA is owner and president of Elliott Valuation & Consulting Services, LLC. Rob is a graduate of the Citadel, the military college of South Carolina. Rob was born and raised in Spartanburg, SC and that is where he resides. Rob has a wife, Andrea, and two daughters, Anna Camille and Greer. Rob is involved in the community and currently serves as a member of the Patrons Society, which volunteers for the Hope Center for Children organization. He is a former board member of the Spartanburg Young Professionals, which is a function of the Chamber of Commerce. He is also an active member of First Presbyterian Church of Spartanburg.
